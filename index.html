About me

My current focus is on participatory approaches to AI. I am also interested AI Ethics, Critical AI, and reasoning about the impacts of automated decision making systems on social systems.

I am currently affiliated with University of Michigan CSE as a PhD student. Outside of research, I organize with my graduate researchers union, GEO-3550.

My colleagues and I coordinate a political economy and algorithms reading group. We are sponsored by the Rackham Graduate School at the University of Michigan. Visit our website to join.
